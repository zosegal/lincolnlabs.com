The NSA vs. Individual Privacy

Hear from the Snowden's former family lawyer

October 3rd
Hosted at Raven Bar
1151 Folsom Street

Is the NSA really trampling on our Constitution and civil liberties?

Hundreds of thousands of files released by Edward Snodwen exposed a massive program to mine private citizen information at an unpredecented scale. While some disagree on the merits and honor of Snowden's release, exposing such a big secret has started the discussion on the state of our privacy and civil rights when it comes to online information.

Watch this conversation with Bruce Fein, former Snowden Family Lawyer and Constitutional & International Law Scholar, and hear his perspective on the state of our civil liberties and internet privacy.
